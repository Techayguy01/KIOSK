a. Touch‑first

* Minimum touch targets: 48px–64px
* Large, breathable spacing
* Zero small text links
* No hover‑only interactions (kiosks don’t have mice)

b. Glanceability

* Screens must be readable at 1 meter distance
* Large headings
* High contrast
* Minimal choices per screen (3–5 max)

c. Guided flow (wizard‑style UI)

* Users are stressed, tired, or foreign travelers
* Every screen should answer:
“What am I supposed to do right now?”

d. Zero distractions

* No complex navigation menu
* No ads, no non‑essential UI
* No scrolling unless absolutely necessary

e. Accessibility / inclusivity

* Multi‑language
* Text-to-speech & speech input (if possible)
* Clear iconography
* Dark + light modes optional

f. Error‑free design

* Choose > type: dropdowns, selectors, QR scan
* Defensive UI: prevent invalid input
* Automatic validation after every step
* Back button on every screen

g. Time‑based safety

* Auto‑reset session after X seconds
* Locked-down kiosk mode
* Never show previous guest’s data on return
